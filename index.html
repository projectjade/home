AWARENESS

Did You Know

In Singapore, an estimated 5.6% of the population are affected by depression during their lifetime. Yet, there has not been a real solution to help people cope with depression, other than medication.

We seek to design a healthy alternative that would act as a rehabilitative site that can also allow people with depression to voice their feelings and emotions. This site would also act as a site to raise awareness about people with depression so that people would not view them as mad, disgruntled people but people whom we can welcome and include in society.

When feelings of intense sadness -- including feeling helpless, hopeless, and worthless-- last for many days to weeks and keep you from functioning normally, your depression may be something more than sadness. It may very well be clinical depression-- a medical condition that needs to be treated.

However, many people do not believe in talking to their counsellors or family/friends, thinking that they are not trained like psychologists and will be unable to help with their depression. Another ineffective solution are antidepressants, which have been proven to have numerous side effects.
